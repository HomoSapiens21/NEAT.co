  • 01 Who We Are?

    The NEAT company comprises a family of leading real estate brokers in an evolving market, mortgage companies, settlement service providers, insurance companies, relocation companies and affiliated businesses. We are the owners of the Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices and Real Living Real Estate franchise networks.

  • 02 What We Do?

    NEAT is important. It represents the boundaries of our most personal aspirations and for many, represents the largest transaction in their lives. But the experience of buying and selling a home can be difficult. Confusing. And laden with complexity. When we began in 1998, our goal was to change that. We developed a strategy that integrates all the pieces of the real estate transaction puzzle including mortgage, title, escrow, insurance and relocation into a suite of home services that work together. Today, while our companies each operate autonomously, they share the unifying priority of delivering an exceptional experience to customers throughout the home buying transaction process.

  • 03 Industry Leadership

    NEAT has been at the forefront for advocating real estate brokers to retain and control their listing data. HomeServices has, and will continue to play a key leadership role in shaping the organizational structure of Upstream RE and Broker Public Portal and in attracting industry participants.
